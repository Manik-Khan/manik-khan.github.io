How to practice
1 Start the drone and listen to the full tanpura sound.
2 Mute reference strings to isolate the one you want to match.
3 Turn on your string and adjust the slider until the beating slows and disappears.
4 Unmute the reference and compare. Matching Sa three times in a row reveals everything.
